Clinicians

Ameena Isa, MD is a board certified psychiatrist who has vast experience with adults, children and adolescents. She has wide ranging experience in diagnosing and treating psychiatric disorders including but not limited to depression,post traumatic stress disorder, anxiety and mood disorders, ADHD, autism spectrum disorders and developmental delays.She completed her Psychiatry residency at Texas Tech University, Lubbock, Texas and her fellowship in Child and Adolescent Psychiatry at the University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center of Dallas, Texas.


Cristin Dooley, Ph.D is a licensed psychologist who previously held a professorial position at the University of Texas Southwestern Medical Center of Dallas working at the institution's Autism Center. She obtained her doctorate from Texas Women's University along with a minor in School Neuropsychology. Dr. Dooley has broad experience in developing evaluation and therapy programs for children with autism spectrum disorders.Her experience inludes extensive work with Independent School Districts providing psychological consultations to children and adolescents. She has been an active member of the American Psychological Association since 1995 and the Regional Association for School Psychologists since 1999.


Joane Davis is an experienced licensed professional counselor with a track record of success providing counseling to children, adolescents and adults in education, corrections, and community outpatient settings. She has been recognized for her outstanding communication and interpersonal skills and the ability to successfully advocate for children and families. She is a nationally certified counselor (NCC) and a current member of the American Counseling Association and Texas Counseling Assocation. Joane holds a Master's degree in Counseling from Webster University in Lakeland, Florida.
